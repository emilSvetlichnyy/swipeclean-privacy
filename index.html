Privacy Policy

Last updated: 05.03.2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application "Swipeo" (the “App”) handles user information.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. General Information

We respect your privacy and strive to minimize the collection and processing of data. The App is designed so that its core functionality operates locally on the user's device, without transmitting personal data to external servers, except as explicitly described in this Policy.

2. Information We Access

2.1 Access to Photos and Videos

The App may request permission to access your photos and videos solely to provide features such as viewing, sorting, and deleting media files at the user’s request.

The App does not upload, store, or transmit your photos or videos to external servers.

2.2 Personal Data

The App does not collect or store:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts
  • Photo or video content

2.3 Technical Information

The App may collect limited technical information necessary to ensure stable operation and improve the performance of the App. This may include:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App interaction events
  • Crash and performance data

This information is collected in aggregated or anonymized form and is used solely to improve the App's stability, performance, and user experience.

Some technical and usage information may be processed by third-party analytics providers as described in Section 6.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

The App may offer paid subscriptions. All payments are processed exclusively through the Apple App Store using Apple’s payment infrastructure.

We do not collect, store, or have access to payment card details or billing information. Payment processing is governed by Apple’s own privacy policy.

4. Data Storage

All user settings and data related to the App’s functionality (such as sorting preferences or interface options) are stored locally on the user’s device.

You may delete this data at any time by removing the App from your device.

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell or trade user data.

However, the App may share limited technical or analytics information with trusted third-party service providers strictly for the purposes of:

  • App analytics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Improving application stability

These providers process information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data may also be disclosed if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

6. Analytics and Tracking

The App uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to better understand how users interact with the App and to improve its functionality and user experience.

Firebase Analytics may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • App usage events
  • Interactions with app features

This data is processed in aggregated form and is not intended to directly identify users.

Firebase Analytics does not allow us to personally identify individual users.

For more information about how Google processes data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Security

We take reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect user data. However, no method of data storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. User Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Request information about data processing
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict data processing

Since most App data is stored locally on the device, many requests can be fulfilled by deleting the App from your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version becomes effective upon publication within the App or on this page.

We recommend reviewing this Policy periodically.
